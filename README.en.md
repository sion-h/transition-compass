# Transition Compass

**Transition Compass** is an integrated guide service designed to systematically compile information on transgender rights and transition-related procedures in South Korea, allowing individuals to access necessary information and processes in one place.

## üß≠ Project Purpose

This project was created to reduce information inequality and support individuals in living self-directed lives.

* **Unified Information Hub**: Centralized access to administrative, medical, and psychological guidelines  
* **Efficient Checklist System**: Visualization of required examinations and preparation steps  
* **Well-being First**: Prioritizing psychological and physical health and overall quality of life

This project is not a space for ideological or philosophical debate and strictly adheres to the following principles:
